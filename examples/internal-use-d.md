# Internal-use-D example

This example shows the strongest internal-use reciprocity posture. It requires
public release of the exact internally used covered work, materially integrated
internal systems, and the non-secret operational materials needed to reproduce
substantially equivalent combined operation.

## Good fit

- Projects that want maximal transparency for internal deployments
- Stewards that view integrated internal systems as part of the reciprocal deal

## Bad fit

- Organizations with substantial proprietary internal integrations
- Projects seeking a lighter internal-use compromise
